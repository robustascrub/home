About Us

Robusta Scrub

Soap | Scrub | Environmentally Friendly

Robusta Scrub is a category of manufacturing and applied technology business in the cosmetics sector with an initial stage based on the utilization of local commodities and a strong commitment to environmental sustainability. Located in Sekarbela, Mataram, this business focuses on using environmentally friendly raw materials, especially coffee grounds from collaboration with Kopi shop in Mataram city, several local coffee shops that have so far only been disposed of as organic waste. Through circular economy innovation, the coffee grounds are processed into a mixture of raw materials for making bar soap that has natural exfoliating properties in the Robusta Scrub branded bar soap product, thus significantly reducing the volume of organic waste in the Mataram area.
